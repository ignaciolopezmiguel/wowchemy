## Abstract

I was invited to give a talk at CERN about my latest work on learning rules to test and improve deep RL agents. This work was published at IJCAI'25 and ECAI'25. We engaged in very interesting experiences and was invited to give a similar talk at RL4AA'26 workshop in Liverpool, UK.
