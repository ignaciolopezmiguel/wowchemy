Ignacio D. Lopez-Miguel

PhD student

Technical University of Vienna (TU Wien)

I am a Ph.D. student at the Technical University of Vienna (TU Wien) under Ezio Bartocci and Agata Ciabattoni. My research focuses on combining logic and formal methods with machine learning. I am also really interested in safe artificial intelligence, enabling the usage of machine-learning based systems into safety-critical applications. Besides my research, I worked in different international organizations, the last one being the European Organization for Nuclear Research (CERN).

I served as sub-reviewer for QEST2023, SPIN2023, FM2023, TACAS2023, VMCAI2024, AAAI2026, and artifact reviewer for CAV2023.
