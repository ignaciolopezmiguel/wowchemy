I am a Ph.D. student at the Technical University of Vienna (TU Wien) under Ezio Bartocci and Agata Ciabattoni. My research focuses on safe artificial intelligence, specifically applying logic to reinforcement learning algorithms. Besides my research, I worked in different international organizations, the last one being the European Organization for Nuclear Research (CERN).

I serve as sub-reviewer for FM2023, TACAS2023, and artifact reviewer for CAV2023.
